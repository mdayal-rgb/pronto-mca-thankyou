# Pronto Cares MCA Brand Spec

Source: `/Users/mdayal/Downloads/Pronto - Brand Guidelines.pdf`

## Brand identity
- Brand: Pronto / Pronto Cares (food-rescue program)
- Positioning line: "Food as medicine - do no harm."
- Core promise: warm, friendly, trustworthy delivery with safety checks

## Color tokens
- Primary (Pronto Orange): `#FF6A1A`
- Accent (Ember): `#E8590C`
- Body text (Cocoa Ink): `#241E1A`
- Background neutral (Cream): `#FFF6EF`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

Functional-only colors (operational UI, not decorative email accents):
- Safe: `#2FA36B`
- Watch: `#E8A317`
- Pull: `#D64545`

## Typography guidance (mapped for MCA)
Brand guide fonts:
- Display/Headings: Fredoka (500-600)
- Body/UI: Nunito (400-800)

MCA brand font family fallback:
- `arial` (closest available practical fallback for warm, rounded sans feel)

## Voice and tone
- Warm: neighborly, reassuring, human
- Trustworthy: precise and calm on safety language
- Clear: short sentences, one idea at a time
- Accessibility: readable for seniors and screen readers

Preferred style:
- "Your meal is on the way... I've checked it's within safe temperature."

Avoid:
- Clinical jargon, internal IDs, procedural acronyms, alarmist wording

## Thank-you email intent
- Audience: donor who gave food support
- Goal: acknowledge gift quickly, show concrete impact, invite continued support
- Content emphasis: gratitude + dignity + safe delivery mission
